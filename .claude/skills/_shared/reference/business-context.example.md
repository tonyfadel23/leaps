# Business Context (template)

Copy this to `business-context.md` (same folder) and fill it in for your
company and role, then keep it updated. Your filled-in version is **gitignored**
(it may contain internal specifics); this `.example` is the shared template.

The pipeline skills and the live-discovery agent read this file to ground every
question in YOUR business and YOUR role — not generic product theory. Run
`/setup` to scaffold it interactively.

## Decision lens (how this PM thinks)
The `pm-proxy` and `pm-challenger` agents answer and stress-test in a senior-PM
voice calibrated to this lens. Describe how you make calls:
- **Reframe first?** [do you reframe the problem before jumping to solutions?]
- **Evidence bar:** [what evidence you require before claiming high conviction]
- **Default stance:** [opinionated/decisive vs. exploratory]
- **Pet peeves:** [the weak arguments you always push back on]
