# Feasibility Page Template

Output template for `pipeline/{idea-slug}/feasibility.md` (brief-page source for
the Feasibility slide). Keep the scoring formula transparent.

---

```markdown
## Feasibility: [idea-slug]

### Score

**[X.X] / 5** — [one-line read: ship-ready / needs work / blocked]

Formula: `feasibility = mean(component scores)`, each component scored 1-5 on
buildability × evidence. Show the inputs, never just the result.

| Component | Score (1-5) | Evidence | Notes |
|-----------|-------------|----------|-------|
| [data availability] | [n] | [explore/table that exists] | … |
| [integration]       | [n] | [API/system check]        | … |
| [ops/process]       | [n] | [who runs it]             | … |

### Dependencies

| Dependency | System / Owner | Status | Blocking? |
|------------|----------------|--------|-----------|
| [dep]      | [owner]        | [ready/in-flight/missing] | [yes/no] |

### Key Risks

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| [risk] | [high/med/low] | [action] |

### Verdict

[Buildable as-is / buildable with the named mitigations / blocked until X.]

---
*Generated by /4 prove, {date}*
```
